I’m Lu, and I organised this mini online exhibition.

When I first opened up submissions for Torn Leaf, I really wasn’t sure what to expect. I didn’t know what types of things would get submitted, or if there would be any submissions at all. Thankfully, I feel fortunate enough to say that lots of wonderful people sent in their creations, and they were all so lovely that I felt a lot of pressure in doing them justice. I hope you enjoy browsing them below.

I encourage you to go slow. Take your time, listen to or read what people have to say along the way. If you leave this website and come back another time, it should remember where you were so that you can carry on from there.
