Torn leaf

Torn leaf is an experimental gallery that publishes all sorts of things.

  • art
  • music
  • essays
  • papers
  • anything

My goal is to bring together different communities that wouldn't normally cross paths. It's a place where big names and small names can combine their efforts, and make something beautiful.

I'll accept a huge range of submissions, from the serious to the silly — as long as you put your heart into it and express yourself.

If your work is usually serious and academic, this is a chance for you to do something more creative. If your work is small and lighthearted, this is a chance for you to do something big and important. Here are some words for your moodboard:

  • good
  • short
  • surrealist
  • no ideas
  • express yourself

Format

Your submission needs the following things.

  • Your full name.
  • A photo of your face.
  • The title of your submission.
  • An audio recording of you reading the content of your submission (ad libs are allowed). If your submission contains something that can't be read, describe that part.
  • The content of your submission. This must also contain your photo, full name, submission title, and audio recording.
  • Instructions on how you want your submission to be displayed (eg: background colour, font, layout, max width, any images, interactive parts, etc). You can write this down in words, or you can send an image/video of a mockup. If you know how, you can write some markdown and/or HTML and/or CSS and/or javascript, and I'll try to make it work.

If your submission is accepted, it will be published under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.
